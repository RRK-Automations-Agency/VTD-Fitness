Founder Story

Inturi Rekha

Founder, VTD Fitness Solutions Private Limited

Every strong institution is built on the foundation of resilience, discipline, and vision. The journey of Inturi Rekha stands as a true reflection of these values.

Beginning her professional path in the field of powerlifting, Rekha demonstrated exceptional dedication and consistency from an early stage in her career. Through relentless training, unwavering focus, and a commitment to excellence, she quickly rose through the ranks in competitive powerlifting.

Her journey was not defined by shortcuts, but by discipline — early mornings, intense training sessions, and a continuous pursuit of improvement. Over the years, she established herself as a formidable athlete, earning recognition at district, state, and national levels.

As her career progressed, Rekha expanded her role beyond personal achievement. She took on leadership responsibilities in the fitness and sports ecosystem, contributing to the growth and promotion of powerlifting in Telangana.

"This vision led to the foundation of VTD Fitness Solutions Private Limited — a company built with the mission of transforming fitness services through structure, accessibility, and professionalism."

Her story continues to inspire a new generation of athletes and entrepreneurs — proving that with vision, consistency, and hard work, it is possible to build something meaningful and impactful.

Core Imperatives

Operational Code

Mission

At VTD Fitness Solutions Private Limited, our mission is to create a structured and accessible fitness ecosystem that nurtures strength, discipline, and excellence among individuals across all levels. We aim to provide professional guidance, modern training systems, and opportunities that help athletes and fitness enthusiasts achieve their highest potential.

Vision

Our vision, inspired by our founder Inturi Rekha, is to build a strong and healthy nation by developing high-quality fitness profiles, with a special focus on empowering talent in Telangana and across India. We strive to uplift women in fitness and sports, encouraging confidence, independence, and leadership through strength-based training.

Values

Our core values are rooted in discipline, consistency, integrity, and empowerment. We believe in leading by example, creating equal opportunities, and fostering a culture where hard work and determination transform individuals into champions, both in fitness and in life.
